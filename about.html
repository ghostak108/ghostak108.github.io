The duo

Two minds. One sonic language.

ZweiKlang merges ghostak’s cinematic techno storytelling with Artemis’ organic, experimental flair. Together we craft soundtracks for sunsets, rooftops, and dancefloors.

From the Black Forest to Croatia’s beaches, we perform immersive sets packed with slow-burning builds, euphoric peaks, and raw emotions. Every show is tailored to the location, the crowd, and the moment in time.
